Project Overview

This project involves the development of a news article classifier that categorises articles based on their content. Utilising a dataset of 20 articles sourced from the Sky News website, the classifier distinguishes between two distinct categories: one focusing on scientific topics, exemplified by articles such as "Osiris-Rexâ€™s sample from asteroid Bennu will reveal secrets of our solar system," and another on cryptocurrency, illustrated by articles like "Bitcoin slides to five-month low amid wider sell-off." The classification is achieved through an analysis of semantic similarity using the Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency (TF-IDF) embedding method.

Skills: HTML Parsing, Text Processing (Cleaning, Lematisation, Stop Words Removal), TF-IDF Implementation, Cosine Similarity Measurement, Java Programming, Statistical Analysis.
